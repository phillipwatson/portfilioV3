PWDesign

Hey, I'm Phillip, an anime and film enthusiast,seeking a career that aligns
with my current interests and skills. At nineteen I moved out of my parent's
home and began to experience a new world, one I was fearful and curious about.
I began to craft the life I wanted to live. I had recently started a small film studio.
With that my love for film and anime led to an appreciation for Japanese design sense.
I then moved from Charlotte NC to Washington D.C with my siblings. Having a taste of independence made me anxious to get back to designing my own life.
While studying filmmakers and designers, I learned skills essential to the design profession.

I gained experience defining project scope, negotiating and learning about users' goals.
As well as how to shape a useful product experience. I then decided that a career in design
could be what I needed. Effective design shapes the aesthetics, interactions and experiences of our world.
I will use design to explore philosophical questions of how and why we live the way we do.
With this I will convince people to reflect on an individual level. My experiences have trained
me to deliver content a skill that lies at the heart of design.

The idea of essentialism; the right content, in the right place, at the right time.
